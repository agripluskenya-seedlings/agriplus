Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you deliver?

We provide efficient countrywide delivery services across Kenya. Whether you are farming in Central, Rift Valley, Eastern, Western, or Coast region, we make arrangements to ensure your consignment reaches your closest dynamic transit point or town safely.

How is the seedling delivered?

To ensure maximum survival rates, all seedlings are securely packaged in structured organic crates or custom bundles that protect the delicate root system and soil plugs during transportation. Larger orders are dispatched via vetted public transport couriers, dedicated agro-logistics partners, or custom farm pick-up routing based on your order capacity.
