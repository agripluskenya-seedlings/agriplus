Our Seedling Catalog

We propagate certified, high-yield varieties optimized for different ecological zones in Kenya.

1. High-Value Fruit Trees

High Demand

Grafted Pixie Orange

Sweet, seedless, and golden-orange fruits. Highly drought-resistant and perfectly suited for semi-arid areas like Ukambani, Coast, and parts of Rift Valley.

Export Grade

Grafted Hass Avocado

A-grade grafted seedlings utilizing robust local rootstocks. Exceptional oil content, high demand for European and Asian export markets.

Popular Fruit

Apple Mango Grafts

Rich yellow-red skins, fiber-free sweet flesh, and large fruit sizing. Highly popular in local Kenyan open markets and juice processing industries.

Premium Selection

Washington Navel Mango

An exotic, heavy-bearing green-to-orange mango variety. Renowned for its unique aromatic flavor profiling and superb shipping durability.

2. Commercial Cash Crops

Long-term Wealth

Grafted Macadamia Nuts

Early-bearing, low-maintenance, high-kernel-recovery varieties (Murang'a 20 / EMB-1). Excellent passive income generator for medium-to-high altitudes.

Certified Cash Crop

Ruiru 11 / Batian Coffee

High-density planting varieties featuring absolute immunity to Coffee Berry Disease (CBD) and Leaf Rust. Reduces spraying costs significantly.
